Naruto

Creator: Masashi Kishimoto

Genre: Action / Adventure

Streaming: Netflix, Crunchyroll

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Synopsis: Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a sealed demon fox spirit inside him, strives to become the strongest ninja and earn the respect of his village. Naruto is a massively popular Japanese anime/manga franchise (1999–2017) centering on Naruto Uzumaki, an ostracized, ramen-loving orphan ninja aiming to become Hokage to earn village respect. The series, animated by Pierrot, spans two main parts (Naruto and Shippuden) totaling 720+ episodes, featuring intense battles, deep character development, and themes of friendship, loneliness, and perseverance.

Attack on Titan

Creator: Hajime Isayama

Genre: Dark Fantasy

Streaming: Netflix

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Synopsis: In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid Titans, Eren Yeager and his friends join the military to fight back and uncover the truth behind the Titans' existence. Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a critically acclaimed Japanese anime/manga series (2009–2021) created by Hajime Isayama. Set in a dystopian world where humanity is trapped within walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight back against the Titans and uncover dark secrets about their origins. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and thought-provoking themes of freedom, sacrifice, and human nature.

Death Note

Creator: Tsugumi Ohba

Genre: Thriller

Streaming: Netflix

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Synopsis: Light Yagami, a high school student, discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it. He embarks on a mission to create a utopia free of crime, while being pursued by the genius detective L. Death Note is a psychological thriller anime/manga series (2003–2006) created by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata. The story follows Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who gains possession of a supernatural notebook called the "Death Note," which allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. As Light uses the Death Note to eliminate criminals and create a utopia under his rule, he becomes the target of a mysterious and highly intelligent detective known only as "L." The series explores themes of morality, justice, and the consequences of absolute power. Death Note is widely regarded as one of the greatest anime/manga series of all time, praised for its intricate plot, complex characters, and philosophical depth.
